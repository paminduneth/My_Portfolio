Hand tracking system

I developed a hand tracking system utilizing OpenCV and MediaPipe. This system captures video from a camera and processes the frames to detect hand landmarks in real-time. It visualizes the detected landmarks and their connections on the video feed, and displays the frame rate for performance monitoring.

Voloume Hand Controll system

I developed a hand tracking system that enables real-time gesture-controlled volume adjustment. This system uses OpenCV and a custom hand tracking module to capture video from a webcam and detect hand landmarks. By measuring the distance between the thumb and index finger, the system adjusts the volume level accordingly. The volume is represented visually on a bar on the screen.
